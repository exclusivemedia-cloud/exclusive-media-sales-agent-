# Offer Reference — Revenue Operations & Enterprise Acquisition Blueprint

Use this as the factual/tone source when drafting outreach. Do not invent numbers,
deliverables, or terms beyond what's here. Personalize only the framing (industry,
pain point, business name) — never the price or deliverables.

PREPARED BY: Exclusive Media

## Objective
Transition the prospect's company from one-off residential bookings into
high-margin commercial contracts and high-density residential routes, via a
fully automated, omni-channel inbound lead engine and automated B2B outreach
infrastructure.

## Phase 1 — Local Residential Domination & Lead Velocity
- Localized content engine: 3 short-form videos/week for local algorithmic reach
  (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook).
- Omni-channel lead capture: Meta + Google + website chat integrated into an
  automated SMS booking engine, 24/7 instant capture.
- Local search velocity: automated post-job review system to push Google Maps /
  Google Business visibility.

## Phase 2 — Commercial B2B Acquisition System
- Property manager targeting: custom data scraping + outbound systems aimed at
  local facility managers, corporate parks, property management firms.
- High-authority B2B content: 2 commercial case studies + LinkedIn multi-channel
  campaigns/week, positioning the prospect as an enterprise vendor.
- Premium proposal infrastructure: custom design assets + standardized digital
  proposal templates for winning commercial bids.

## Revenue Ops & Data Infrastructure
- Fully managed data integration mapping all inquiries to the prospect's CRM.
- Performance dashboard: CPL, lead-to-booking ratio, marketing ROI attribution.
- Sub-90-second automated, context-aware AI text response to every lead
  ("frictional lead gap" elimination).

## Investment
- $5,000/mo, month-to-month, no long-term lock-in.
- Anchor value: one mid-tier commercial property management or corporate plaza
  contract typically yields $20,000–$50,000+ ARR. One commercial contract per
  quarter covers the entire retainer; residential bookings alongside it are
  pure surplus margin.

## Scale Acceleration Milestone
- Once fleet integration / route density are maximized, path to a $10,000/mo
  Autonomous Enterprise Tier: custom backend AI databases, automated team
  scheduling logic, internal client support infrastructure — running the
  business independent of manual oversight.

## Personalization notes for outreach
- {{business_name}} — the prospect's company name, from the GHL contact record.
- {{contact_name}} — first name of the person who submitted the inquiry.
- {{industry}} — their service type (e.g. moving, cleaning, lawn care, HVAC).
  Use it to make Phase 1/Phase 2 concrete for their world (e.g. for a cleaning
  company: "corporate plazas and office parks needing recurring janitorial
  contracts" rather than generic "commercial contracts").
- Keep email under ~200 words. Keep SMS under 320 characters, one clear CTA
  (book a call), no pricing in the SMS.
- Never state the $5,000/mo figure or ARR anchor numbers in the SMS — save
  pricing detail for the email/call, not a first-touch text.
